Molecular models for periodic surfaces and nanoparticles

The first step to set up a molecular simulation is to carefully define your system. At the DFT some of the most used models intend to represent heterogeneous systems of small isolated nanoparticles or a section of an extended surface to be computed under periodic boundary conditions.

This app was created to aid the set up of molecular simulations by providing molecular models (atomic and cell coordinates) of these kinds of systems.
